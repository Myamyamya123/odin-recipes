Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Penne Noodles
  • Chopped bacon - Or real bacon bits if you're in a hurry
  • Mushrooms - Fresh or canned.
  • Chopped keilbasa sausage
  • Minced garlic - Fresh (5 cloves) or jarred (1 tbs)
  • Heavy cream
  • mozzerella cheese - Fresh (grated) or jarred
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • (Optional) Other seasonings to taste
  • Grated cheese of your choice
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. place water in a pot with salt and bring to a boil.
  2. While water is boiling chop keilbasa into 1/4 inch pieces.
  3. When water gets to a rolling boil add noodles to pot.
  4. In a second pot, melt 1 tablespoon of butter with garlic, keilbasa, bacon, and mushrooms
  5. Let items simmer in pot at medium heat until fragrent and keilbasa is lightly charred.
  6. Deglaze with heavy cream and add in mozzerella cheese.
  7. Let sauce get to a rolling boil on medium heat, add seasonings to taste.
  8. Once pasta is done, drain all but a half cup of pasta water and let stand until sauce is ready.
  9. When sauce is boiling add one cup of grated cheese (Or more to taste).
  10. Pour pasta sauce into noodles, stir until well combined and add in fresh parsley. If pasta looks a little dry, add in pasta water.
  11. Once combined, serve with garlic bread or a side salad.
