Loaded Nachoes

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tortilla chips - Homemade or store bought
  • Salsa - Flavor/heat level is your own choice
  • Sour Cream
  • Nacho Cheese
  • Lettuce (Shredded)
  • Tomatos (Diced)
  • Guacamole - Homemade or store bought
  • Grated cheese of choice

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan set to medium-high heat.
  2. Drain grease and add salsa and taco seasoning to ground beef.
  3. Once beef looks juicy and tastes good you're ready to assemble.
  4. Start with a base layer of beef.
  5. Top beef with nacho cheese.
  6. Top cheese with salsa.
  7. Top salsa with lettuce.
  8. Top lettuce with tomatos.
  9. Top tomatoes with guacamole.
  10. Top guacamole with grated cheese of choice.
  11. Top cheese with sour cream in a zig-zag pattern.
  12. Serve with tortilla chips on the side.
